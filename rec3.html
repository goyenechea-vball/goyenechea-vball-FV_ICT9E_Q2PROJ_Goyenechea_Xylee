    Turon Ingredients Steps
    -6 pieces bananas saba or plantains, cut in half (lengthwise)
    -1 cup jackfruit ripe, sliced
    -1 1/2 cup sugar
    -12 pieces lumpia wrapper
    -2 cups cooking oil


    		1. Roll banana on a plate with sugar. Make sure that the banana is covered with enough sugar.
    2. Place that banana on a lumpia wrapper. Add a slice of ripe jackfruit on top (Note: make as much as you want).
    3. Fold and Lock the wrapper, use water to seal the edge.
    4. Heat the oil in a pan and add the sugar. Watch for the brown sugar to float.
    5. Place the wrapped banana inside and cook until the wrapper is golden brown.
    6. Done and Serve!
