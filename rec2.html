    Sinigang na Hipon Ingredients Steps
    -1 lb. shrimp cleaned
    -44 grams Knorr Sinigang sa Sampaloc Mix
    -1 bunch kangkong
    -15 pieces snake beans
    -5 pieces okra
    -1 piece eggplant
    -1 cup daikon radish sliced
    -1 piece tomato sliced
    -3 pieces long green pepper
    -1 piece onion
    -2 quarts water
    -Fish sauce and ground black pepper to taste


    		1. In a cooking pot, boil water. Add onion, tomato, and radish. Cover then boil for 8 mins
    2. Add shrip then cook for 1 min.
    3. Add Knorr Sinigang sa Sampaloc Recipe Mix. Stir until it completely dissolves. Cover then cook for 3 mins.
    4. Add long green pepper, snake beans, okra, and eggplant. Stir and cook for 5 mins.
    5. Add kangkong stalks into the pot. Season with fish salt and ground pepper.
    6. Add kangkong leaves then cook for 1 min.
    7. Done and Serve!
