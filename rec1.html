    Ginataang Mais Ingredients Steps
    -2 cups coconut cream
    -2 cups coconut milk
    -1/2 cup glutinous rice malagkit
    -1 cup sweet corn chopped
    -1/2 to 3/4 cup granulated white sugar
    -teaspoon vanilla extract
    -a pinch of salt


    		1. Boil coconut milk and coconut cream in a pot.
    2.Once it starts boiling, add glutinous rice then stir for 10 mins.
    3. Add sugar then stir.
    4. Add corn then stir. Continue to cook in low heat for 8 mins. (Note: can cook longer to make it softer)
    5. Add vanilla extract and salt then stir.
    6. Done and Serve!
